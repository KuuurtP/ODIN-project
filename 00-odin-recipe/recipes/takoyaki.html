Takoyaki

What is Takoyaki?

is a popular Japanese street food consisting of small balls of batter filled with octopus, tempura scraps, pickled ginger, and green onions.

They are cooked in special molds and often topped with takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, bonito flakes, and seaweed flakes.

Ingredients

  • Takoyaki batter
  • Octopus
  • Tempura scraps
  • Pickled ginger
  • Green onions
  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bonito flakes
  • Seaweed flakes

Steps:

  1. Prepare ingredients: Cut octopus into small pieces. Combine tempura scraps, pickled ginger, and green onions.
  2. Heat takoyaki plate: Heat a takoyaki plate over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter: Fill each mold with takoyaki batter about halfway.
  4. Add fillings: Add octopus, tempura scraps, pickled ginger, and green onions to each mold.
  5. Cook takoyaki: Cook until the bottom is golden brown. Use a takoyaki pick to gently turn the balls halfway.
  6. Cook until done: Continue cooking until the takoyaki balls are cooked through and golden brown.
  7. Top with toppings: Drizzle with takoyaki sauce and mayonnaise. Sprinkle with bonito flakes and seaweed flakes. Serve immediately.
