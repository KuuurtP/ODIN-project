Sushi

What is Sushi?

is a Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice combined with a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, vegetables, or egg, often wrapped in seaweed (Nori).

It's known for its fresh flavors and delicate presentation.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fillings (various options, such as salmon, tuna, shrimp, avocado, cucumber, imitation crab)
  • Wasabi
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook sushi rice: Follow package instructions for cooking sushi rice.
  2. Prepare sushi rice: In a large bowl, combine cooked rice with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Use a rice paddle to gently fold the ingredients together until the rice is evenly coated and cooled.
  3. Assemble sushi: Place a nori sheet on a sushi rolling mat with the shiny side down. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving a small space at the top.
  4. Add fillings: Place your desired fillings along the bottom edge of the rice.
  5. Roll sushi: Use the sushi mat to carefully roll the sushi into a tight log. Moisten the top edge of the nori with water to seal.
  6. Cut sushi: Using a sharp knife, cut the sushi roll into desired sizes.
  7. Serve: Serve sushi with soy sauce and wasabi.
